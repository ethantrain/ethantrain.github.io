Other Important

Cantaloupe is also known as mushmelon, rockmelon, sweet melon, or spanspek.

The most important members of the Cucurbitaceae are squash, pumpkin, zucchini, some gourds, watermelon, and cucumber.

Fun Fact!

The luffa or loofah, the sponge used as a body scrub, is actually a dried out Cucurbitaceae called the loofah.
